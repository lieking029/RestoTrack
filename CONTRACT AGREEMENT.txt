8. SCOPE AND DELIMITATIONS 

Scope of the Study 

This study focuses on the development and implementation of RestoTrack for Hapag sa Marikina, covering the following features: 

Web Application 

Admin Module 

Designed to help administrators manage restaurant operations efficiently. 

Administrator 

User Management - Add, edit, remove, or archive employee accounts. 

Employee Accounts - Includes personal information, job details, and employment status and history. 

Update contact details and profile information when necessary. 

Sales Reports - Generates reports to help track business performance. 

Sales Analytics 

Track sales data to determine which dishes are the most and least popular. 

Generate reports showing daily, weekly, and monthly trends. 

Implement a dashboard that displays best-selling items in real time. 

Generate reports that restaurant owners can access anytime. 

Real-Time Inventory Tracking 

Stock levels update automatically when items are sold. 

Track slow and fast-moving items using the EOQ method. 

Menu Management 

Update, add, remove, or archive menu items. 

Mark items as unavailable when out of stock. 

Smart Waste Management & Expiry Date Tracking 

Sends alerts to the owner and manager for expiring ingredients, preventing spoilage. 

Mobile Application 

Employee Module 

Designed for staff members to assist them in handling orders, payments, and food preparation in a timely manner. 

Barista and Server 

Order Menu - An ordering module for baristas and servers to send orders directly to the kitchen and cashier, improving communication and reducing manual errors. 

Cashier 

Point of Sale System - POS System Upgrade – Faster transactions, better sales reports, and automatic stock updates. 

Login - A login module for cashier. 

Cook & Chef 

Kitchen Display System (KDS) - Orders will appear on a screen in the kitchen to avoid mix-ups. 

Delimitation 

The system will be customized for Hapag sa Marikina and may require modifications for other businesses. 

Does not include customer-facing applications for placing orders (e.g., mobile app for customers). 

Limited to restaurant operations and does not extend to supply chain management beyond inventory tracking. 

Requires internet connectivity for full functionality, though offline mode will support basic transactions. 

B-Tree Indexing will be used primarily for sales and inventory data, and other indexing methods will not be explored in-depth.
